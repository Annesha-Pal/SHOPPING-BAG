Frequently Asked questions

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, UPI, and net banking. All transactions are securely processed.

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order from your account under "My Orders."

Orders can be canceled or modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order may already be processed or shipped. Contact our support team as soon as possible.

We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.

Standard delivery usually takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout.
